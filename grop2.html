What is ICT Strand

Information:

  • The ICT strand in Senior High School in the Philippines is a specialized academic program that focuses on information and communications technology. It prepares students for careers and further studies in technology-related fields by imparting knowledge and skills in areas such as programming, networking, web development, and database management. The curriculum combines theoretical concepts with hands-on activities to develop problem-solving and critical thinking abilities. Students learn about information systems, technology applications, and their societal impact. The ICT strand enables students to pursue higher education in computer science and related fields or enter the workforce directly. It equips them with the necessary competencies to navigate the digital landscape effectively.

Benifit of being ict

  • The ICT (Information and Communications Technology) strand in Senior High School in the Philippines focuses on technology, computer science, and information systems. Students learn subjects such as programming, database management, web development, networking, multimedia design, information systems, IT ethics and laws, and emerging technologies. The curriculum may vary between schools. The ICT strand equips students with skills and knowledge in technology and prepares them for higher education or careers in technology-related fields.
